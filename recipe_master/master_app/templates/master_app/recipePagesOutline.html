Salad

This dish is easy to make and fun to eat. Perfect for those hot summer days or as a quick lunch. This regular, mary-sue salad can't do you wrong!

Ingredients:

  • Lettuce
  • Chopped Tomatoes
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Croutons
  • Parmesan Cheese

Directions:

  1. Place the lettuce and tomatoes in a bowl
  2. Add the ranch dressing onto the lettuce and tomatoes
  3. Sprinkle the croutons on top
  4. Add as much parmesan cheese as you like
  5. Enjoy!
